1. 1. Introduction

    At NOKABZAA, we prioritize the security and privacy of your property documents. This policy outlines the measures we take to handle, share, and securely destroy documents to ensure their safety and confidentiality.

  2. 2. Document Collection and Upload

    • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for uploading their property documents via the NOKABZAA app. Documents include, but are not limited to, NALA Certificate, Airport NOC, Fire NOC, Environmental Clearance, Municipal Approval, Labour Cess, TS RERA Approval, and TS RERA Number.
    • Secure Upload: The app uses end-to-end encryption to protect documents during the upload process. This ensures that documents are secure from interception and unauthorized access.

  3. 3. Document Storage

    • Encrypted Storage: All uploaded documents are stored in encrypted form on our secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption algorithms to ensure that documents are only accessible by authorized personnel and systems.
    • Access Control: Access to stored documents is strictly controlled and limited to authorized NOKABZAA personnel who require it for verification and monitoring purposes. Each access request is logged and monitored for security purposes.

  4. 4. Document Sharing

    • Secure Sharing: When documents need to be shared with third parties (e.g., legal verification), we use secure sharing methods such as encrypted email or secure file transfer protocols. Access to shared documents is limited to authorized recipients only.
    • User Consent: Documents are only shared with third parties with the explicit consent of the user. Users are informed about the purpose of sharing and the identity of the third party.

  5. 5. Document Use

    • Confidentiality: All documents are handled with the utmost confidentiality. NOKABZAA personnel are trained to follow strict confidentiality protocols to prevent unauthorized disclosure or misuse of documents.
    • Limited Use: Documents are used solely for the purpose of property verification, legal compliance, and monitoring services. They are not used for any other purposes without user consent.

  6. 6. Document Destruction

    • Retention Policy: Documents are retained for a specified period as required by law or until they are no longer needed for the purpose for which they were collected. After this period, documents are securely destroyed.
    • Secure Destruction: Physical documents are shredded using high-security shredders. Electronic documents are securely deleted using data wiping methods that meet industry standards to ensure that they cannot be recovered or reconstructed.

  7. 7. Shredding and Disposal Procedures

    • Shredding: Physical documents are shredded using cross-cut shredders, which cut documents into small, unreadable pieces. This method ensures that shredded documents cannot be reconstructed.
    • Electronic Disposal: Electronic documents are deleted using secure wiping software that overwrites the data multiple times, making it unrecoverable. This process ensures that deleted documents cannot be accessed or restored by any means.
    • Verification: The shredding and disposal processes are verified and logged by authorized personnel to ensure compliance with our document destruction policy.

  8. 8. User Rights

    • Access and Correction: Users have the right to access their stored documents and request corrections if any inaccuracies are found. Requests can be made through the NOKABZAA app or by contacting our customer support team.
    • Deletion Requests: Users can request the deletion of their documents at any time. Upon request, documents will be securely deleted in accordance with our document destruction procedures.

  9. 9. Compliance and Audits

    • Legal Compliance: NOKABZAA complies with all relevant laws and regulations governing document handling, storage, sharing, and destruction. This includes data protection and privacy laws.
    • Regular Audits: We conduct regular audits of our document handling processes to ensure compliance with our policies and identify areas for improvement. Audits are conducted by internal and external auditors.

    This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the NOKABZAA app and our website.
